A unique and hilarious show with a storyline all about you!

Rhinoceros perform improvised comedy which, in a nutshell, is a form of comedy theatre in which the plot, characters and dialogue of the show are made upon the spot. The performers tie together multiple scenes culminating in a full-length show… And it’s all about you!

Each show is skilfully crafted to reflect your big day.

We will creatively incorporate your company values and objectives as well as any other distinct messages into a show, providing you with a unique and hilarious experience.

We can structure the production around you and your type of event, whether you’re hosting a large company function, award ceremony, Christmas do, cocktail party, or intimate gathering.
