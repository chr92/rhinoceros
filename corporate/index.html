A unique and hilarious show with a storyline all about you!

Rhinoceros perform improvised comedy which, in a nutshell is a form of comedy theatre in which the plot, characters and dialogue of the show are made upon the spot. The performers tie together multiple scenes culminating in a full-length show… which is all about you!

Each show is skilfully crafted to your bespoke requirements. We will creatively incorporate your company values and message as well as any other distinct specifications into a show, providing you with a unique and hilarious experience.

We can structure the production around you and your type of event, whether you’re hosting a large company event, award ceremony, Christmas doo, cocktail party, or intimate gathering.
