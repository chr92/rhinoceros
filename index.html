Rhinoceros Improv

Rhinoceros are a team of long form improvisers who have been thrilling and inspiring audiences across the comedy circuit for over two years. We perform witty scenes and comedy characters on the back of a one-word suggestion. It’s like watching an episode of Friends but without the fancy hair-dos and nice tooshies.

Every month we host ‘Rhinoceros’ Big Friday, an all-star night of character comedy and improv with some of London’s finest guest comedians. With a different line-up each month of improv, character and sketch comedy, each show has a unique feel to it.

Our bond, energy and desire to make up hilarious improv is infectious and fun.

The team are made up of actors, writers and comedians, some of whom have appeared on BBC TV, ITV & BBC Radio 4. We’re all trained up to the hilt by the Free Association and continue to learn and grow from our constant focus and desire to perform fun and exciting improv.

Rhinoceros’ BIG Friday

Rhinoceros host an all-stars comedy extravaganza featuring some of London’s finest comedians every last Friday of the month.

The Rhinos present a new and exciting line up of improv, character and sketch comedy every month. You’ll never be bored again.

If you don’t have a great time we’ll give you your crisps back.
